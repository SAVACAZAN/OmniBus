ℹ️ About This Generator

BIP-39 + BIP-44: Industry standard for HD wallet derivation. Same standard used by Ledger, Trezor, MetaMask, etc.

Derivation Path: m/44'/coin_type'/account'/change/index
Example: m/44'/60'/0'/0/0 = Ethereum address #0

Security: This page works completely offline. All derivation happens in your browser. Your seed is never uploaded anywhere.

Like BitAddress.org: This is OmniBus's version of the famous BitAddress generator, extended to support 7+ blockchains and post-quantum cryptography (ML-DSA).
